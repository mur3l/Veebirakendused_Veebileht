About

About Pedal Faster:

Welcome to Pedal Faster, the modern bicycle magazine dedicated to the world of cycling enthusiasts. Our mission is to bring you the latest trends, reviews, and insights into the ever-evolving world of bicycles. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or just starting out, Pedal Faster is your go-to source for everything related to bikes.

At Pedal Faster, we believe in the beauty and simplicity of cycling. Our content covers a wide range of topics, from in-depth product reviews and brand histories to innovative advancements in cycle manufacture and repair. We also feature engaging articles on cycling culture, touring diaries, and the best cycling videos on the web.

Our team of passionate writers and photographers is committed to providing high-quality, informative, and entertaining content. We strive to inspire our readers to explore the world on two wheels and to appreciate the craftsmanship and design that goes into every bicycle.

Join us on this journey and stay up-to-date with the latest news, trends, and stories from the cycling community. Pedal Faster is more than just a magazine; it's a celebration of the cycling lifestyle.
